Central Park

Central Park is one of the most famous parks in the world. It is the most visited urban park in the United States and is the oldest public park in the United States. You walk your dog, have a picnic, bike, or just go on a stroll yourself.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

The Met is one of the most famous museums in the world! It is located on 84th street and fifth avenue. It carries art from many different time periods and locations inlcuding:

  • Ancient Egypt
  • Medieval
  • Ancient Greek and Roman

JG Melon

JG Melon is one of my favorite places to eat in New York City. It has some of the best burgers I have had and is a fun place to go with friends. It has a nice atmosphere as it is always lively. The only thing is that it only takes cash so make sure you bring some with you.

Natural History Museum

As an engineer, I love science. This is why the Natural History Museum was always a favorite. It gives the history of the world through the lens of science which I enjoy. Additionally, there are many interactive demos throughout the museum to use which add to the enjoyment. There are many dinosaur skeletons and realisitic looking animal figures to learn about as well as human history.
